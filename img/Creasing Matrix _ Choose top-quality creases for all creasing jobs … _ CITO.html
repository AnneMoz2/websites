Creasing Matrix

Choose top-quality creases for all creasing jobs …

... with ULTIMATE, DYNAMIC, BASICplus and PLAST creasing matrix you have the widest product range worldwide of creasing systems at your disposal. You will find exactly the right solution for every application and material.

With CITO you obtain optimum creasing quality with the best price-performance ratio. You can trust in CITO, the world’s market leader: With CITO as your partner you can be sure of perfect creasing. (CITO System in Use)

CITO ULTIMATE

The unique high performing film-based creasing matrix. The flexible coating with a sturdy base enables a consistently high crease quality from the first to the last sheet – even for extremely large runs. The first creasing system with ISEGA certificate.

more

CITO DYNAMIC

The efficient film-based creasing matrix. Great value for money.

more

CITO BASICplus

For high-quality creasing of folding cartons to displays through to corrugated board packaging. Ideal for smaller and medium runs.

more

CITO PLAST

The classic metal-based creasing matrix with plastic shoulders for cylinders and platens.
