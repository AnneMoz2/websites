CITO ULTIMATE

Introducing CITO ULTIMATE, the unrivaled film-based creasing matrix with a flexible surface, boasting the prestigious ISEGA certificate. Recognized globally as the leading creasing matrix, CITO ULTIMATE offers unparalleled size variety for diverse applications—from virgin fiber to recycled material, paper to folding cartons, laminated microflute to corrugated board. With the Fingerlift technique for perfect handling, EasyFix technology for residue-free removal, and color-coded profiles, it ensures precise identification, preventing size mix-ups. Now certified by ISEGA, it meets EU Directive standards for food contact materials, making it ideal for food packaging, including extremely large runs. Experience excellence in crease quality, crack reduction, and versatility across 600 sizes and versions.

The economical film-based creasing matrix with plastic shoulders. Perfect for high-quality creasing in folding cartons, displays, and corrugated board packaging. Featuring the Fingerlift technique for easy handling and knife trimming, plus EasyFix technology for residue-free removal. Color-coded for quick identification, it ensures user-friendly design, chamfered edges for flawless sheet transfer, and is ideal for smaller to medium runs. CITO PLAST
Presenting CITO PLAST—the classic metal-based creasing matrix with plastic shoulders, tailored for precision creasing on cylinders and platens. Engineered for folding cartons, corrugated board packaging, and displays, it ensures perfect handling through the Fingerlift technique. Enjoy outstanding adhesion on the cutting plate and residue-free removal, courtesy of EasyFix technology. With a patented full-surface join ensuring additional safety, user-friendly color coding, and availability in various versions, CITO PLAST is ideal for small to medium runs. Experience the pinnacle of creasing technology with this innovative solution.

CITO DYNAMIC
Introducing CITO DYNAMIC, the efficient film-based creasing matrix designed for optimal performance across solid board, corrugated board, display, and folding carton board applications. With the Fingerlift technique for seamless handling and EasyFix technology for residue-free removal, it excels in medium to long runs. Available in versatile widths of 10 mm, 12 mm, and 15 mm, tailored to specific creasing channels, CITO DYNAMIC offers exceptional value for money. Elevate your creasing efficiency with this dynamic solution that combines precision, adaptability, and cost-effectiveness in one.
