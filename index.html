Our Story

Printing Equipment & Materials (Private) Ltd was established in 1995. We are a partner in the packaging industry for Cutting and Creasing solutions for boxboard and corrugated board. We provide TOP QUALITY Tools & Accessories of world renowned brands for our customers who are striving for excellence in quality and productivity. Our Product portfolio offer a broad choice for Die Makers, Die Cutters & Printers covering Box line, Form line, Print line and Process line.
